## The one-line brief
Dark cosmic stage + editorial magazine masthead + seven planet personas. Warm darks (paper cream on black, not blue-black). Plus Jakarta Sans for display, Newsreader italic for pull-quotes, IBM Plex Mono for labels. Emoji as persona anchors. No blue-purple gradients. No em-dashes in copy. "Schrödinger's Boyfriend" energy — half physics, half dating-app.

## Key files
- `colors_and_type.css` — tokens
- `ui_kits/portfolio/` — React/JSX components (Masthead, PlanetDock, PersonaView, CompactLogo, Starfield)
- `assets/` — logo + starfield + any icons
- `preview/` — small self-contained demo cards per concept
